THE PHILOSOPHY OF ART

WHEN GOOD BECOMES POWERLESS, IT TRANSFORMS INTO EVIL

A boundary is not merely a physical limit but also a psychological one. Childhood and maturation, friendship and love – all these represent borders that define our experiences and constrain our interactions.
Everything possesses untarnished boundaries that should not be breached according to societal norms and morality. In the philosophy of Oscar Wilde, we observe that white symbolizes unity and innocence, while black is associated with sin and evil. These two colors always remain unblended, creating a transparent veil that hinders the merging of the two forces.
